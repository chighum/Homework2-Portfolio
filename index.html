About Me

I was born and raised in Denver, CO as the middle of eight children in my family. Growing up I played a lot of sports, mainly focusing on soccer, lacrosse, and skiing. In 2015, I moved to Austin, TX to attend the University of Texas. While I was mainly focusing on my studies, I was also able to continue my competitive lacrosse career by joining the UT club lacrosse team. After graduating from UT in 2019 with a B.S. in Petroleum Engineering, I moved to Vail, CO in order to become a ski instructor. After two seasons of full time ski instructing and landscaping during the offseason, I recently moved back down to Denver in order to pursue a full-time, year-round career in web development.

Aside from skiing, which is far and away my favorite hobby, I enjoy a variety of outdoor activities here in Colorado, including fishing, hiking, and camping. I'm a huge fan of all Denver sports, especially the Broncos and Nuggets, and also enjoy watching Premier League and Champions League European football. I am also an avid world traveler, and you can see a collection of photos I have taken during my travels to places such as Argentina, Thailand, and Italy on this page. Lastly, I greatly enjoy listening to music, mostly the Grateful Dead but also Tedeschi Trucks Band, Tyler Childers, Sturgill Simpson, and Mac Miller. While it will always make me a little sad to have missed Jerry Garcia, my favorite activity today is attending Dead & Company concerts to hear the live music of the Dead.
