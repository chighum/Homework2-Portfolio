About Me

I was born and raised in Denver, CO before moving to Austin, TX to attend the University of Texas. After graduating from UT in 2019 with a B.S. in Petroleum Enginneer, I moved to Vail, CO in order to become a ski instructor. After two seasons of full time ski instructing and landscaping during the offseason, I have recently moved back down to Denver in order to pursue a full-time career in web development. Aside from skiing, which is far and away my favorite hobby, I enjoy a variety of outdoor activities here in Colorado, including fishing, hiking, and camping. I am also an avid world traveler, and you can see a collection of photos from places like Argentina, Thailand, and Italy on this page. Lastly, I greatly enjoy listening to live music and attending concerts, especially Dead & Company and other Grateful Dead cover bands.
